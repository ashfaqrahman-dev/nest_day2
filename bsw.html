Course Overview

The Bachelor of Social Work (BSW) program equips students with knowledge and practical skills needed to address social problems, support vulnerable communities, and promote social justice. Students participate in field work, community projects, and social research to gain real-world experience.

Course Curriculum

  • Human Growth and Development
  • Social Psychology

  • Community Organization
  • Social Research
  • Family and Child Welfare
